`2026 May 31`

The gap between a business owner's capability and the automation they need is smaller than most consulting pitches present it to be. [No-code automation platforms](disclaimer.md) — Zapier, Make, n8n — have been connecting business software together without code for over a decade. What changed in 2026 is that these platforms added AI capability as native functions: a Zapier step can now call a language model mid-workflow, extract structured data from a document, generate a draft, or classify an input. The assembly is visual and the technical barrier for a motivated non-developer is genuinely low. [n8n specifically](disclaimer.md) is the tool most relevant to a practitioner building repeatable automation for clients: source-available, self-hostable, hundreds of integrations, deployable in a way the client can own and maintain. [WhatsApp Business API automation](disclaimer.md) is particularly relevant for SME clients in markets where WhatsApp is the primary business communication channel — [Nigeria put 35 ministries on WhatsApp](disclaimer.md) using exactly this kind of pipeline, and the pattern transfers directly.

A first SME engagement does not require building a custom AI system. In most cases, the highest-value first automation is a connection between two tools the business already uses, with a language model inserted at the step where a human is currently reading something and deciding something. That insertion is the automation: the model reads the email, classifies the request, fills the form, routes the case. The barrier is not the technology — it is identifying which specific connection to make and committing to building it. A consultant who can find that connection in a morning is delivering more value than any vendor demo.
