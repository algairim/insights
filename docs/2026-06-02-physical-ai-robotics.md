`2026 June 2`

For three years the AI conversation lived on a screen — a chat box, a document, an inbox. That is starting to change.

[Physical Intelligence is raising around $1bn](disclaimer.md) to build foundation models for robots — the same architecture behind language models, pointed at movement. [FANUC and Google put Gemini into 1.1 million industrial robots](disclaimer.md). [Siemens deployed a humanoid on a working factory floor](disclaimer.md). [Foxconn and Nvidia are planning humanoids on an AI-server line](disclaimer.md). The thread connecting them is that the model which learned to predict text is being retrained to predict action. For most small businesses this is not next quarter's problem — humanoids on a factory floor are still capital-heavy and rare. But the direction matters for anyone whose work was assumed safe because it is physical. The screen was never the boundary. It was just where the technology started.
